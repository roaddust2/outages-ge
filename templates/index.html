About

Hi there, I'm Mark. Having lived in Georgia, I know firsthand how disruptive infrastructure outages can be, especially when working from home. That's why I created this app — it consolidates public outage data, making it easier to track everything in one place. Plus, you can subscribe to outage notifications via a Telegram bot.


Best part? It's completely free, now and in the future.
